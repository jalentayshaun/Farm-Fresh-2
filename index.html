Our Mission

At Farm Fresh Gainz, it is our mission to provide the highest quality protein sources to fuel your body and aid in muscle growth and recovery. We support and love all sports and fitness journeys from professional athletes to someone just trying to be better than they were the day before. Our passion is watching people’s confidence and smile grow as they progress on their way to their goal. We are all athletes at some level here at FFG and believe in what we do and sell. Our meats are raised on Canadian farms, free of hormones and grass fed. We believe that to perform at our highest potential, the fuel we put in our bodies must be of the utmost quality.
