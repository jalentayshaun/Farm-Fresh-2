I recently returned from an intense 7-week training program at the renowned Tiger Muay Thai gym in Phuket, Thailand. Alongside my passion for martial arts, I’m proud to serve as a member of the Canadian Armed Forces.

My fitness journey began in my youth, with a love for sports and physical activity. Achieving your best self isn’t just about working out—it’s about fueling your body the right way. With my expertise, we’ll ensure you’re eating to optimize your results while training to become stronger, leaner, and healthier. I believe in a holistic approach that combines both the mind and body for lasting change.
